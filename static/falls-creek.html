Falls Creek Deliveries

Ski Season Only.

Monday-Friday: Order by 1pm for same day 4pm delivery.

Prescriptions written by Falls Creek medical centre

Prescription will be faxed from the Medical Centre to the Mount Beauty Pharmacy.

The Medical Centre will require the following details:

  • A CURRENT (in date) Medicare Card Number, including the sub-numerate (number beside family member) and expiry date.
  • A valid Pension/Concession Card number & expiry date.
  • Credit Card details
  • Contact phone number (preferably mobile) or Lodge number.
  • An on-mountain address.

If received before 1pm, the items will be delivered to the Medical Centre by 4pm that day, where they may be collected.

Other Prescriptions

For other prescriptions please:

  1. Please fax the following to Mount Beauty Pharmacy on (03) 5754 1550
    • The prescription(s) AND DUPLICATE.
    • Completed ‘Falls Creek Orders’ form (available from your lodge or Resort Office).
  2. Please sign all prescription and repeat forms and leave at Resort Office.
  3. Please advise where you would like repeats posted.
If preferred, you may call the pharmacy directly with these details on (03) 5754 4224.

Please Note: Legislation requires you contact our Pharmacist on 5754 4224 to discuss the use of any prescription or ‘Pharmacist Only’ medicine (eg some cold & pain preparations). Failure to do so will mean these items cannot be supplied.

Other Items

For other non-prescription items please fax a completed ‘Falls Creek Orders’ form (available from your lodge or Resort Office) to Mount Beauty Pharmacy on (03) 5754 1550. If preferred, you may call the pharmacy directly with these details on (03) 5754 4224.

Delivery

All items NOT ordered by the Falls Creek Medical Centre will be delivered to the Falls Creek Resort Management office – upstairs above the day shelter (near Falls Express Lift) by 4pm (if ordered by 1pm). The Office CLOSES at 5PM. You will be required to provide proof of identity.

Freight

There will be a freight/handling fee of ten dollars ($10) per parcel delivered to either the Falls Creek Medical Centre or the Resort Management office. This will be charged to your credit card.
